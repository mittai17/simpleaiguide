AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the field of computer science that focuses on building systems that can perform tasks which normally require human intelligence.

## Why AI matters

- It powers tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot.
- It automates repetitive tasks.
- It helps in healthcare, finance, education and more.

## Types of AI (high level)

- Narrow AI
- General AI (theoretical)
- Superintelligent AI (theoretical)
